## Tips for Best Results

### Do This
- **Specify the platform** â€” Each platform has different best practices
- **Share your voice** â€” Personal posts need personality context
- **Include the goal** â€” Engagement vs clicks vs brand awareness
- **Provide key points** â€” What message must come through?

### Avoid This
- **Cross-posting identical content** â€” Each platform needs adaptation
- **Ignoring hooks** â€” First line determines whether people keep reading
- **Too many CTAs** â€” Social is for engagement, not hard selling
- **Forgetting visuals** â€” Recommendations help you prepare assets

### Pro Tips
1. **Request hook options** â€” Test different angles
2. **Ask for platform-specific formatting** â€” Line breaks, emojis, structure
3. **Include hashtag strategy** â€” Right hashtags expand reach
4. **Get a content mix** â€” Thought leadership + product + engagement

## Platform Best Practices

| Platform | Character Limit | Best Practices |
|----------|-----------------|----------------|
| LinkedIn | 3,000 | Hook in first line, line breaks, end with question |

| TikTok | 300 (caption) | Short captions, let video do the talking |
