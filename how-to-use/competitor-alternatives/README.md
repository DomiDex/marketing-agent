# /competitor-alternatives

Create competitor comparison and alternative pages.

## Overview

**What it does:** Helps create SEO-optimized comparison pages—"[Product] vs [Competitor]" and "[Competitor] alternatives"—designed to capture high-intent search traffic.

**When to use it:**
- Creating "[Your Product] vs [Competitor]" pages
- Building "[Competitor] alternatives" pages
- Designing comparison landing pages
- Planning comparison content strategy
- Writing fair, compelling comparison copy

**What you'll get:**
- Page structure and content outline
- Comparison copy that's honest yet persuasive
- Feature comparison tables
- SEO optimization guidance
- Fair treatment of competitors

---

## How to Invoke

**Command:** `/competitor-alternatives`

**Trigger phrases:**
- "Create a vs page..."
- "[Competitor] alternatives page..."
- "Comparison page for..."
- "Compare us to [Competitor]..."
- "Alternative to [Competitor]..."

## Tips for Best Results

### Do This
- **Be honest** — Acknowledge where competitors are better
- **Know your audience** — Why are they searching this?
- **Use specifics** — Real features, real prices
- **Include social proof** — Testimonials from switchers

### Avoid This
- **Bashing competitors** — Unfair criticism hurts credibility
- **Outdated info** — Keep pricing and features current
- **Feature-only focus** — Benefits matter more than checkboxes
- **Ignoring SEO** — Target real search keywords

### Pro Tips
1. **Interview switchers** — Best content comes from real customers
2. **Update regularly** — Competitors change, so should pages
3. **Link between comparisons** — Build internal authority
4. **Test conversion paths** — Different CTAs for different intents
