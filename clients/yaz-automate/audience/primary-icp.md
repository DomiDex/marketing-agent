> The main audience we create content for — SMB owners who want AI but lack technical expertise.

---

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Job Title** | Business Owner, Founder, Operations Manager |

## Demographics

### Role & Responsibilities

**Typical titles:**
- Business Owner
- Founder / Co-founder
- Operations Manager
- Office Manager

**Reports to:** Themselves (owners) or CEO

**Key responsibilities:**
- Running day-to-day operations
- Managing customer relationships
- Growing the business
- Wearing multiple hats

### Company Characteristics

**Company size:** 1-50 employees

**Industry verticals:**
- Service businesses (consultants, agencies)
- E-commerce
- Professional services
- Local businesses scaling up

**Company stage:** Established enough to invest in tools, but not so big they have IT staff

**Geography:** US, UK, Canada, Australia primarily

**Tech-savviness:** Mainstream — they use tools but aren't technical

---

## Pain Points

### Primary Pain (Most Urgent)

**Pain:** Drowning in repetitive tasks that take time away from growing the business

**Impact:** Can't focus on strategy, working long hours, growth stalls

**Current workaround:** Doing it manually, hiring staff, trying (and failing with) Zapier

**In their words:** "I'm spending all my time on admin work instead of actually growing the business"

### Secondary Pains

| Pain Point | Impact | Priority |
|------------|--------|----------|
| "I know AI could help but don't know where to start" | Missing opportunity, paralyzed by options | High |
| "I've tried tools like Zapier but couldn't get them to work" | Wasted time, frustration, back to manual | High |
| "I can't respond to leads fast enough" | Lost sales, competitors win | High |
| "I need to scale but can't afford more staff" | Growth limited by capacity | Medium |

---

## Goals & Desired Outcomes

### Primary Goal

**Goal:** Free up time from repetitive tasks to focus on growing the business

**Metric:** Hours saved per week, revenue growth

**Timeline:** Want results quickly (weeks, not months)

### Other Goals

| Goal | Metric | Priority |
|------|--------|----------|
| Respond to leads faster | Response time, lead conversion | High |
| Scale without hiring | Revenue per employee | High |
| Work fewer hours | Work-life balance | Medium |
| Look more professional | Customer experience | Medium |

## Day in the Life

**A typical day includes:**
- Checking email and responding to inquiries
- Following up with leads and customers
- Managing team and operations
- Doing admin tasks that "only I can do"
- Working evenings to catch up

**Tools they use daily:**
- Email (Gmail, Outlook)
- Calendar (Google Calendar)
- Maybe a CRM (HubSpot, Pipedrive)
- Spreadsheets (Google Sheets, Excel)
- Communication (Slack, text, phone)

**Meetings & stakeholders:**
- Client meetings
- Team check-ins
- Sales calls

**Stressors:**
- Not enough time in the day
- Things falling through the cracks
- Can't scale without hiring
- Competitors who seem more together

---

## Buying Behavior

### Triggers

*What causes them to start looking for a solution?*

- A particularly bad week of being overwhelmed
- Losing a deal because they responded too slowly
- Hearing about a competitor using AI successfully
- Reading an article about AI for small business
- Someone mentions automation in conversation

### Evaluation Criteria

| Criteria | Importance | Notes |
|----------|------------|-------|
| Ease of use / Done-for-me | High | Don't want another tool to manage |
| Clear ROI | High | Need to justify spend |
| Trustworthiness | High | Will this actually work? |
| Price | Medium | Have budget if value is clear |
| Speed to results | Medium | Want quick wins |

### Decision Process

**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks from awareness to purchase

**Decision makers:** Often just them (owner)

**Approval process:** Internal decision, may discuss with partner/spouse or key employee
