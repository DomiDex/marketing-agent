## Pricing Tiers (Draft)

### Starter — $2,000-3,000

**Target user:** SMB owner ready for first automation

**Positioning:** Get started with proven, high-impact automation

**Includes:**
- Workflow assessment
- One AI agent (lead response OR appointment booking)
- Basic integrations (2-3 tools)
- Setup and testing
- Training and handoff
- 30 days support

**Best for:** First-time automation, clear use case

### Growth — $5,000-7,000

**Target user:** Business ready to automate multiple workflows

**Positioning:** Scale your automation for bigger time savings

**Includes everything in Starter, plus:**
- 2-3 AI agents
- More complex integrations
- Custom workflows
- 60 days support

**Best for:** Multiple automation opportunities, scaling

### Custom — $7,000-10,000+

**Target user:** Complex or unique needs

**Positioning:** Tailored solution for your specific business

**Includes everything in Growth, plus:**
- Fully custom development
- Complex integrations
- Ongoing maintenance options
- Extended support

**Best for:** Unique workflows, complex requirements

## Value Metric

**What drives pricing:** Complexity, number of agents, integration requirements

**How it scales:** More agents and complexity = higher investment

## Pricing Objections

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "Too expensive" | "Let's calculate the ROI — how much is your time worth? How much are you losing to slow responses?" |
| "Competitor is cheaper" | "Are they doing everything for you, or expecting you to figure it out? Our price includes the strategy, build, and support." |
| "Need to see ROI first" | "That's fair. Let's start with a workflow assessment to identify your highest-impact opportunity." |
