## Core Product Description

Yaz Automate builds custom AI agents that automate repetitive business tasks for SMBs. Unlike DIY automation tools that require technical skills, or expensive development teams that speak in jargon, Yaz Automate handles everything from strategy to implementation — explaining it all in plain English.

Services include:
- Workflow automation (connecting systems, automating handoffs)
- Sales/marketing agents (lead response, appointment booking)
- Customer support agents (FAQ handling, ticket routing)
- Custom AI solutions (tailored to specific business needs)

## Use Cases

### Use Case 1: Service Business Lead Follow-up

**Scenario:** A consulting firm gets leads from their website but can't respond fast enough

**How the product helps:** Lead response agent replies instantly, qualifies leads, books discovery calls

**Example:** Lead submits form at 9pm → gets response in 30 seconds → books call for next day

**Outcome:** 40% more leads converted, no evening work required

### Use Case 2: E-commerce Customer Support

**Scenario:** An online store gets the same questions repeatedly (shipping, returns, sizing)

**How the product helps:** FAQ agent handles common questions automatically

**Example:** Customer asks "Where's my order?" → Agent checks tracking → Provides update

**Outcome:** 60% reduction in support tickets

### Use Case 3: Agency Appointment Scheduling

**Scenario:** A marketing agency spends hours coordinating client meetings

**How the product helps:** Booking agent handles all scheduling, reminders, rescheduling

**Example:** Client wants to book → Agent shows availability → Confirms + sends reminder

**Outcome:** Zero time spent on scheduling
