# Working Process

> How Yaz Automate works with marketing assistance.

---

## Content Approval Process

### Standard Approval Flow

```
[Draft] → [Founder Review] → [Revisions] → [Final Approval] → [Publish]
```

**Step 1: Draft submission**
- Deliver to: Founder
- Format: Markdown / Google Doc

**Step 2: Founder review**
- Timeline: 1-2 business days

**Step 3: Revisions**
- Rounds included: 2

**Step 4: Final approval**
- Approver: Founder

---

## Content Standards

### Quality Standards

- [ ] Clear, jargon-free language
- [ ] Matches brand voice (casual, approachable)
- [ ] Has clear CTA
- [ ] Claims backed by specifics

### Brand Alignment

- [ ] Follows tone guidelines (4/5 casual)
- [ ] Uses approved words, avoids restricted words
- [ ] Consistent with positioning
