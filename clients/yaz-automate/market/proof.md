## Proof Building Plan

### Priority 1: First Client Case Study

**What we need:**
- Successful project completion
- Measurable results
- Client willing to be featured
- Quote and possibly photo

**How to get it:**
- Offer discount for case study participation
- Document everything from day one
- Make it easy for client to participate

### Priority 2: Testimonial Collection

**What we need:**
- 3-5 short quotes
- Name and company attribution
- Permission to use

**How to get it:**
- Ask immediately after successful delivery
- Make it easy (provide prompts)
- Follow up if needed

### Priority 3: Metrics Library

**What we need:**
- Time saved data
- Response time improvements
- Conversion improvements

**How to get it:**
- Build measurement into every project
- Track before/after
- Document consistently
