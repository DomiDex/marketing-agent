# Industry Context

> Market trends and external factors affecting AI automation for SMBs.

---

## Industry Overview

**Industry/Market:** AI Automation for Small Business

**Market size:** Growing rapidly (AI services market projected at $200B+ by 2027)

**Growth rate:** 30%+ CAGR for AI services

**Key players:** OpenAI, Anthropic (AI providers), Zapier, Make (DIY tools), various agencies

---

## Market Trends

### Trend 1: AI Democratization

**What's happening:** AI tools are becoming more accessible to non-technical users. ChatGPT brought AI mainstream.

**Impact on our marketing:** Perfect timing — awareness is high, but implementation gap exists

**Content opportunities:** "AI for business owners who aren't technical"

### Trend 2: SMB Digital Transformation

**What's happening:** Small businesses are increasingly adopting digital tools, accelerated by pandemic

**Impact on our marketing:** Target audience is more receptive to technology solutions

**Content opportunities:** "Next steps after basic digitization"

### Trend 3: Labor Shortage / Rising Costs

**What's happening:** Hiring is expensive and difficult, especially for SMBs

**Impact on our marketing:** Strong positioning for "automate instead of hire"

**Content opportunities:** "Scale without adding headcount"

## Buyer Behavior Shifts

### How Buying is Changing

| Shift | Old Way | New Way | Implication |
|-------|---------|---------|-------------|
| Research | Ask peers, consultants | Search, LinkedIn, YouTube | Need strong online presence |
| Evaluation | RFPs, formal process | Quick calls, demos | Streamlined sales process |
| Decision | Committee | Owner decides | Faster sales cycle possible |
| Trust | Reputation, referrals | Content, social proof | Need testimonials, case studies |
