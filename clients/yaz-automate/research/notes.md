## Voice of Customer (VOC) Insights

*Collected from market research and target audience understanding*

### How They Describe the Problem

- "I know AI could help my business, but I don't know where to start"
- "I'm drowning in repetitive tasks but don't have the technical skills"
- "I've tried tools like Zapier but couldn't get them to work"
- "I'm spending all my time on admin instead of growing the business"

### How They Describe Success

- "I want to focus on what I do best"
- "I need to be able to respond to leads faster"
- "I want to scale without hiring more staff"
- "I want my business to run smoothly even when I'm not there"

## Competitive Observations

### DIY Tools (Zapier, Make)

- High learning curve for non-technical users
- Support is documentation-based, not personal
- Customers often try and fail, then give up

### AI Agencies

- Often enterprise-focused
- Use technical jargon
- Pricing too high for SMBs

---

## Market Research Findings

### Target Market Size

- 30+ million small businesses in US
- Growing awareness of AI
- Implementation gap is the opportunity

### Buyer Behavior

- Research online before talking to anyone
- Trust peer recommendations
- Want quick wins, not multi-year projects
