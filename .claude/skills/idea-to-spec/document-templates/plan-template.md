## Quality Checklist

Before delivering the plan:

- [ ] Strategic context is clear
- [ ] Objectives are specific and measurable (OKRs)
- [ ] Roadmap has phases with milestones
- [ ] Resources and budget are realistic
- [ ] Risks have mitigations
- [ ] Dependencies are identified
- [ ] Success metrics are defined
- [ ] Governance is clear
- [ ] Next steps are actionable
