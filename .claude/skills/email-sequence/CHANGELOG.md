# Email Sequence Skill Changelog

All notable changes to the email-sequence skill are documented here.

## [1.0.0] - 2026-01-23

### Added
- Initial RULES.md with skill-specific standards
- Sequence strategy requirements (trigger, goal, exit conditions, timing)
- Subject line standards (length, patterns, no ALL CAPS/excessive punctuation)
- Preview text requirements (90-140 chars, complements subject)
- Email structure requirements (one purpose, one CTA per email)
- Copy quality standards specific to email (short paragraphs, conversational)
- Personalization standards with fallback requirements
- Anti-patterns with corrective guidance (9 patterns documented)
- Sequence-specific checklists (welcome, nurture, re-engagement, onboarding)
- Output structure for complete sequence deliverables
- Quality checklist combining skill-specific and global rules
- Email length guidelines by email type

### Based On
- SKILL.md email sequence frameworks

- Global rules from `.claude/rules.md`
