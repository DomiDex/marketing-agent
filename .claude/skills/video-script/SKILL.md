# Video Script

## Before Writing

Gather essential context:

### 1. Video Purpose
- What action should viewers take?
- What's the key message?
- Where in the funnel does this sit?
- What problem does the video solve?

### 2. Audience
- Who is the target viewer?
- What do they already know?
- What do they care about?
- Where will they watch this?

### 3. Platform/Format
- Where will this be published? (website, social, YouTube)
- What's the ideal length?
- What style fits? (animated, live action, screen recording)
- Will there be voiceover, on-screen talent, or both?

### 4. Brand Guidelines
- What's the tone? (professional, casual, energetic)
- Are there brand voice guidelines?
- Any specific language to use or avoid?
- Music/visual style preferences?

## Video Types

### 1. Explainer Videos

**Purpose:** Educate viewers on what you do and why it matters
**Length:** 60-120 seconds
**Best For:** Homepage, landing pages, ads

**Structure:**
1. Hook (0-5 sec): Grab attention
2. Problem (5-20 sec): Relatable pain point
3. Solution (20-50 sec): Your product/approach
4. How it works (50-80 sec): Key features (3 max)
5. Proof (80-100 sec): Social proof/results
6. CTA (100-120 sec): Clear next step

### 2. Product Demo Videos

**Purpose:** Show the product in action
**Length:** 2-5 minutes (or shorter focused clips)
**Best For:** Product pages, sales process, YouTube

**Structure:**
1. Context (0-20 sec): What we're showing
2. Workflow walkthrough: Step-by-step
3. Key features: Highlight differentiators
4. Results: What users achieve
5. CTA: Trial, signup, or contact

### 3. Customer Testimonial Videos

**Purpose:** Build trust through social proof
**Length:** 60-90 seconds
**Best For:** Landing pages, case studies, sales

**Structure:**
1. Introduction (0-10 sec): Who they are
2. Challenge (10-30 sec): Problem they faced
3. Solution (30-50 sec): How you helped
4. Results (50-70 sec): Outcomes achieved
5. Recommendation (70-90 sec): Would they recommend?

### 4. Social Media Videos

**Purpose:** Engage, educate, or entertain
**Length:** 15-60 seconds (platform dependent)
**Best For:** LinkedIn, Twitter, TikTok, Instagram

**Platform Guidelines:**
- LinkedIn: Professional, educational, 30-90 sec
- Twitter/X: Quick hits, 15-45 sec
- TikTok: Entertaining, authentic, 15-60 sec
- Instagram Reels: Visual, trendy, 15-60 sec
- YouTube Shorts: Educational hooks, under 60 sec

### 5. Webinar/Tutorial Videos

**Purpose:** Deep education on a topic
**Length:** 10-60 minutes
**Best For:** Lead generation, customer education

### 6. Ad Videos

**Purpose:** Drive specific action
**Length:** 15-30 seconds (paid ads)
**Best For:** YouTube ads, social ads, display

```markdown

- **Length:** [XX seconds]
- **Style:** [Animated/Live action/Screen recording]
- **Voiceover:** [Yes/No, style]
- **Music:** [Style/mood]

---

## SCENE 1: Hook [0:00-0:05]

**VISUAL:**
[Description of what viewers see]

**VOICEOVER/TEXT:**
"[Script]"

**NOTES:**
[Direction for animator/editor]

---

## SCENE 2: Problem [0:05-0:20]

**VISUAL:**
[Description]

**VOICEOVER/TEXT:**
"[Script]"

**NOTES:**
[Direction]

---

[Continue for each scene...]

---

## CTA [Final Scene]

**VISUAL:**
[Logo, CTA button, URL]

**VOICEOVER/TEXT:**
"[Final script with clear CTA]"

**END CARD:**
[What to display]
```

### Demo Video Script Format

```markdown
# [Demo Video Title]

## Overview
- **Length:** [XX minutes]
- **Format:** [Screen recording with voiceover]
- **Goal:** [What viewers should understand]

---

**ON SCREEN:**
[What's visible]

**VOICEOVER:**
"[Script - set context for what we're showing]"

---

## STEP 1: [Action Name] [0:30-1:00]

**ACTION:**
[What the presenter does on screen]

**VOICEOVER:**
"[Explain what we're doing and why]"

**CALLOUT:**
[Any on-screen text or highlight]

---

[Continue for each step...]
```

## Writing Principles

### Hook Immediately
First 3-5 seconds determine if people watch.

**Weak Hook:**
"Hi, I'm John from Acme Corp. Today I want to talk about..."

**Strong Hook:**
"You're wasting 10 hours a week on reports nobody reads."

### Write for Speaking
Scripts are spoken, not read.

- Use contractions (we're, you'll, it's)
- Short sentences (under 15 words ideal)
- Simple words (use, not utilize)
- Conversational tone (like explaining to a friend)

### Show, Don't Tell
Video is visual. Use it.

**Weak:**
"Our dashboard is easy to use."

**Strong:**
[VISUAL: User completing task in 3 clicks]
"Three clicks. Done."

### One Idea at a Time
Don't overwhelm. Each scene = one concept.

### Time Your Script
Rule of thumb: 150 words = 60 seconds
Read aloud to test pacing.

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Website Explainer Videos

**Location:** Homepage hero or landing page
**Length:** 60-120 seconds
**Auto-play:** Usually muted, add captions
**Goal:** Keep visitors, drive trial/demo

### YouTube Videos

**Hook:** First 5 seconds critical (skip button)
**Length:** 2-10 minutes (varies by content)
**Format:** Value-first, CTA at end
**SEO:** Optimize title, description, tags

### LinkedIn Videos

**Format:** Square (1:1) or vertical (4:5)
**Length:** 30-90 seconds
**Captions:** Required (85% watch muted)
**Tone:** Professional, educational
**Hook:** Lead with insight or question

### TikTok/Reels

**Format:** Vertical (9:16)
**Length:** 15-60 seconds
**Captions:** Required
**Tone:** Authentic, less polished OK
**Hook:** Immediateâ€”no intros

### Paid Ads (YouTube/Social)

**Length:** 15-30 seconds
**Hook:** 3 seconds or less
**CTA:** Clear, early, and at end
**Format:** Designed for sound-off

## Testimonial Interview Questions

### Background Questions
- "Tell me about yourself and your role."
- "What does your company do?"

### Problem Questions
- "What challenge were you trying to solve?"
- "What was life like before [Product]?"
- "What had you tried before?"

### Solution Questions
- "How did you discover [Product]?"
- "What made you decide to try it?"
- "How would you describe [Product] to someone who's never heard of it?"

### Results Questions
- "What results have you seen?"
- "What's the biggest change since using [Product]?"
- "Can you share any specific numbers or outcomes?"

### Recommendation Questions
- "Who would you recommend [Product] to?"
- "What would you tell someone considering [Product]?"
- "On a scale of 1-10, how likely would you recommend us?"

## Social Video Hooks

### Question Hooks
- "Did you know that...?"
- "Ever wonder why...?"
- "What if I told you...?"

### Bold Statement Hooks
- "Stop doing [thing everyone does]"
- "[Common belief] is completely wrong"
- "This changed everything for me"

### Curiosity Hooks
- "Here's what nobody tells you about..."
- "The secret to [outcome]..."
- "I discovered something surprising..."

### Pain Point Hooks
- "Tired of [frustration]?"
- "If you're struggling with [problem]..."
- "The #1 mistake [audience] makes..."

### Promise Hooks
- "In 60 seconds, I'll show you..."
- "This one trick will..."
- "Here's how to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]"
